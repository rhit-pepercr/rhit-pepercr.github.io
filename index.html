About

Connor Rhys Peper, born February 22nd 2002, is an American computer science student attending the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. He graduated from Francis Howell North High in 2020 and was proud to be the President of both the FHN Speech/Debate team and the FHN Mock Trial team. In his free time, Connor likes to play video games, read alternate history fiction, and participate in tabletop games with his friends.

Connor Peper has taken Object Oriented Programming and Data Structures and Algorithms. He knows Python, Java, and Javascript, though he's most comfortable in Java.
